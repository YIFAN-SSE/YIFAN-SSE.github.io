Working Paper

Correlated Demand and Beta Dynamics in Sustainable Investing (2025)
Using U.S. active equity fund data from 2012 to 2024, this paper shows that correlated demand from sustainable investors amplifies systematic risk for stocks in their portfolios. I identify stocks persistently overweighted by sustainable investors as experiencing demand shocks. These stocks exhibit significant return beta increases beyond what fundamentals would predict, with effects concentrated during 2016‚Äì2020. I develop a novel measurement capturing the coordination of capital flows across sustainable investors, revealing a demand-driven channel through which thematic investing reshapes systematic risk.
